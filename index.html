Our Heritage

A Family Recipe Passed Down

Nestled in the lush village of Kandanad, Ernakulam, Ammu's Food Products was born out of one family's deep love for Kerala's culinary heritage. What began as cherished kitchen rituals — grinding fresh spices at dawn, hand-rolling Kuzhalappam, and slow-cooking pickles — slowly became something the whole neighbourhood looked forward to.

Friends and family began requesting jars of Mango Pickle, bags of Puttu Podi, and boxes of Chakka Chips to take back home. That quiet joy of sharing traditional food is the heart and soul of everything we make.

Today, we still follow the same traditional recipes — no shortcuts, no preservatives, just the honest flavours of Kerala.
